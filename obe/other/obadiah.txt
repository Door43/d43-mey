====== Obadiah ======

===== Facts: =====

Obadiah was one of Israel's prophets during the time of King Zedekiah.

  * Other prophets at that time were Ezekiel and Daniel.
  * Obadiah prophesied against the people of Edom, who were descendants of Esau.
